Got Questions? We Have Answers.

Find solutions to common inquiries about Peddlesoft. Explore our Frequently Asked Questions to make the most out of your experience and ensure a smooth journey with our comprehensive management platform.

How do I register for membership using Peddlesoft's MMS?

Simply navigate to the Membership Management System (MMS) section and follow the intuitive registration process. Fill in the required details, create your profile, and gain access to a range of membership benefits seamlessly.

Can I customize the external pages in the Content Management System (CMS)?

Yes, Peddlesoft's CMS allows you to effortlessly customize external web pages. Showcase your organization's information, events, and engage members with dynamic content that suits your brand and communication style.

How does Peddlesoft's EMS simplify event management?

Peddlesoft's Event Management System (EMS) streamlines the entire event lifecycle. From planning to post-production, manage conferences, attendance, and social engagement tools effortlessly, optimizing attendee experiences and ensuring successful events.

What features does Peddlesoft's TMS offer for online training programs?

Peddlesoft's Training Management System (TMS) integrates Course Management System (CMS) and Learning Management System (LMS). Experience streamlined course creation, enrollment, communication, and assessment for impactful online training experiences.

How does Peddlesoft's PMS simplify financial transactions?

Peddlesoft's Payment Management System (PMS) simplifies financial transactions with features like invoice generation, payment records management, analytics, and reminders. Experience smooth transactions, accurate records, and valuable financial insights.

Is the Electronic Voting System (EVS) secure for conducting elections?

Yes, Peddlesoft's EVS provides a secure and efficient platform for electronic voting and elections. With digital tools replacing traditional methods, ensure accuracy, transparency, and accessibility for informed decision-making in your organization.
